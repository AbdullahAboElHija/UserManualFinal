How It Works - Video Demo

Watch this demo to see how WorkWithHelmetOnly detects PPE violations in real-time

WorkWithHelmetOnly provides automated PPE detection using advanced computer vision and deep learning. The system offers real-time monitoring, instant violation alerts, comprehensive tracking across video frames, and detailed compliance reporting for construction safety management.

Key Features

  • Automated detection of PPE (helmets) in real-time video streams
  • Instant alerts for safety violations
  • Comprehensive tracking of individuals across video frames
  • Detailed compliance and violation reporting
  • User-friendly dashboard for monitoring and analytics

How to Use

Access the Dashboard
Open the main dashboard to monitor live video feeds and system status.
Upload a Video
Use the upload interface to submit recorded videos for PPE detection and analysis.
View Detection Results
Watch as the system processes the video and highlights detected helmets and violations in real time.
Check Analytics
Navigate to the analytics section to review compliance rates, violation trends, and detailed reports.
Interpret Analytics
Use the visual charts and tables to understand safety performance and identify areas for improvement.
Receive Alerts
Stay informed with instant alerts for any detected safety violations.
